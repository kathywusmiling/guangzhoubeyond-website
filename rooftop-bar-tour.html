What you'll experience

One sky, two completely different cities — caught in the same evening.

🌅

The Sunset Hour

We arrive in golden hour and settle into the best seats in the house — reserved for you in advance. Watch the city slowly catch fire as the sun sinks behind the river and the sky goes through every shade of pink and orange.

🌃

The Night Skyline

Stay on as Guangzhou lights up beneath you. The Canton Tower starts changing colours, the towers along the Pearl River glow neon, and the whole city turns into a slow, sparkling light show — all from 70 floors above.

How the evening unfolds

Easy, glamorous, and timed perfectly so you don't miss a thing.

01

Hotel pick-up
